OUR FUTURE! 🤍

My love,

I want to talk to you about something I think about more than you realize. Not just today, not just this moment between us — but what comes next. The days ahead. The future. Our future. Because when I picture what's coming, when I close my eyes and think about where my life is going, you are not a footnote in that picture. You are the whole thing.

I think about us a lot. More than I let on, more than I probably say out loud on a regular basis. I think about the version of us that exists five years from now, ten years from now — older, a little wiser, having been through things we can't even predict yet. And every single time I imagine that version of us, we are still together. Not because I'm romanticizing it, not because I can't see the hard parts coming, but because I genuinely cannot picture a future that feels right without you in it. You have become so woven into the way I think about my life that removing you from it doesn't even feel like a real option anymore.

I want us to have inside jokes that only make sense after years of history together. I want to know your routines so well that I could navigate your mornings with my eyes closed. I want to be the person you call first when something happens — good news, bad news, something that made you laugh so hard you had to stop walking. I want to earn that place in your life so completely that it becomes second nature to both of us.

I won't pretend otherwise, because pretending would be doing you a disservice. But here is what I can tell you about those chapters: I intend to face every single one of them with you. Not ahead of you, not behind you — beside you. Whatever comes, whatever shifts, whatever life decides to throw at the two of us, I want us to be the kind of team that faces it together and comes out the other side still holding on.

I think about the small futures too, not just the big ones. I think about lazy Sunday mornings that belong entirely to us. I think about the trips we haven't taken yet, the places I want to show you and the places I want you to show me. I think about the meals we'll cook badly and laugh about, the arguments we'll have and resolve and grow from. I think about all the tiny, unremarkable moments that don't make it into any highlight reel but end up being the ones you remember most. I want all of those with you. Every single ordinary one.

When I think about where I want to be, I think about where we want to be. That shift happened quietly, somewhere along the way, and I don't think I've told you that clearly enough. So I'm telling you now. You are in my plans. You are part of what I'm working toward. You are one of the reasons I want to become a better version of myself, because you deserve someone who keeps growing, and I intend to be that person for you.

I also want to assure you that I am not in this carelessly. I am not loving you without thinking about what that means, without considering the weight of it, without understanding that loving someone is a responsibility as much as it is a feeling. I take us seriously. I take your heart seriously. And the future I'm imagining for us is not some vague, floating idea I haven't thought through — it is something I think about with intention, with care, with the full understanding that you are someone worth building something real with.

So when you wonder sometimes if this is going somewhere, if I see a future with you, if you are someone I picture in my long-term life — let me put that question to rest right now, completely and without hesitation. Yes. A thousand times, yes. You are not just someone I love in the present tense. You are someone I am choosing, in the future tense, over and over again before it even arrives.

The future is uncertain for everyone, I know that. None of us can promise exactly how things will unfold, and I won't insult your intelligence by pretending I can control everything that's coming. But what I can promise you is my commitment to showing up for it — for every version of it, the beautiful chapters and the difficult ones — with you beside me. That is the one thing I am sure of, even when everything else is unclear.

I love you, and I am looking forward to every single day of whatever comes next. Not in spite of you being there — because you are there.

The future looks like you. It always has. 🤍.
