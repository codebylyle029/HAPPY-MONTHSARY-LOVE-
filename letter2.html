ASSURANCES

My love,

I want you to read this slowly, because every word here is meant for you — not as something I felt pressured to say, but as something I have been wanting to say, clearly and completely, so that you never have to wonder again.

You are loved. Not in a fleeting, conditional, depends-on-the-day kind of way. You are loved in the kind of way that doesn't need a reason, doesn't come with an expiration date, and doesn't shrink when things get difficult. What I feel for you is steady. It is quiet and sure and it does not waver, even on the days when I don't say it out loud enough. I need you to hold onto that.

I know your mind can be unkind to you sometimes. I know there are moments when a small silence from me turns into a story in your head, when you start wondering if something has changed, if I'm pulling away, if you did something wrong. I want to speak directly to those moments right now: nothing has changed. I am still here. I am still yours. The silence was never about you losing me — it was just life being loud, and I am sorry for every time I let you sit in that uncertainty longer than you should have. You deserve better than that, and I am working on it.

You do not have to be perfect to keep my love. Please let that sink in. You don't have to say the right things every time, don't have to hold yourself together when you're falling apart, don't have to pretend you're okay when you're not. I fell in love with a real person, not a performance. And a real person has hard days, has doubts, has moments of breaking down — and I want to be there for every single one of those moments, not in spite of them, but because of them. Because being there for you in those moments is the part of loving you I take most seriously.

When you feel insecure, come to me. When something is bothering you — even if it feels too small, even if you're afraid I'll think it's irrational — come to me anyway. I would rather you bring me a hundred small worries than carry them quietly alone and let them grow into something heavier. My arms are not just for the good days. They are for the weight too. All of it. I want to hold it with you.

I also want to assure you of something that I think you sometimes forget: you are not a burden to me. Not your emotions, not your needs, not the moments when you need more reassurance than usual. You are not too much. You have never been too much. If anything, I think you ask for far less than you deserve, and I want to be the person who gives you more than you think to ask for. You deserve to feel taken care of. You deserve someone who doesn't make you feel guilty for having needs. And I want to be that for you, every single day.

I am not going to leave. I want to say that as plainly as I possibly can, with no softening and no fine print. I am not going to wake up one day and decide this isn't worth it, because it is. You are. Even on the days that are hard, even on the days we don't quite understand each other — I am still choosing you. That choice is not fragile. It is not something that disappears the moment things get complicated. It is a decision I make every morning, and I will keep making it.

You are safe with me. Your heart is safe with me. I know that trust is not something that can just be declared — it has to be shown, over and over, in small moments and big ones. And I intend to keep showing you. Every day that I show up, every time I reach for you instead of pulling away, every moment I choose honesty over silence — that is me showing you. Pay attention to those moments. Let them be your proof.

There is nothing you could say, nothing you could feel, nothing you could confess about your darkest fears or your worst days that would make me see you differently. I already know you — the real you, the tender and complicated and beautifully human you — and that is exactly the person I love. Not a version of you that has it all together. You, as you are right now, in this very moment. That person is more than enough for me.

So on the days when doubt gets loud, when your chest feels tight and your mind starts spinning with questions I haven't answered yet — I want you to come back to this. Read it again. And remember that somewhere, someone is completely and quietly certain about you. Someone is not confused, not half-in, not looking for a way out. Someone is here, on purpose, because they want to be.

That someone is me. And I am not going anywhere.

I love you. Now, tomorrow, and on every ordinary day in between. 🤍.
