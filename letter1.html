HAPPY MONTHSARRYY!

Happy monthsary, my love.

I still remember the version of me before you — quieter in some ways, more guarded in others, not quite sure what it meant to be truly seen by someone. I had grown used to keeping certain parts of myself tucked away, thinking that was just how things had to be. I didn't expect anyone to reach for those parts gently, without forcing the door open. But then you came along.

You didn't arrive with grand declarations or demands. You came quietly, patiently, the way morning light finds its way into a room even through closed curtains. And somehow, without me fully realizing it was happening, you made space for yourself in my life like you were always meant to be there. Looking back now, another month later, I realize how much has changed simply because you decided to stay.

There were days I wasn't easy to love. Days I was distant, caught up in my own head, unsure how to explain what I was feeling even to myself. There were misunderstandings, small silences that could have easily become bigger ones. And yet, every single time, you stayed. You didn't use those moments as reasons to leave — you used them as reasons to understand me better.

That's the part that humbles me most, if I'm honest. Anyone can love someone on the good days, when everything is easy and laughter comes naturally. But you loved me on the ordinary days too — the tired ones, the uncertain ones, the days that didn't ask to be remembered. And it was in those unremarkable moments that I understood what we had was something rare.

As I sit here thinking about everything that's brought us to this point, I find myself noticing you all over again — really noticing you, the way you deserve to be noticed. The way your eyes soften when you're truly listening, like nothing else in the room matters. The way you laugh without thinking about it, unfiltered and honest, like you've forgotten to be careful around me.

I notice the quiet strength you carry too — how you keep going even on days that are heavy, and somehow still find room to check on me in the middle of it. You give more than you realize, and you rarely ask for anything in return. You are beautiful in the obvious ways, the ones anyone could see from across a room. But you're even more beautiful in the ways only someone paying close attention would ever catch.

I think about your kindness most of all — not the performative kind, but the quiet, steady kind that shows up in small actions. The way you remember things I mentioned once in passing. The way you make people feel comfortable just by being near them. I've watched you do this for others, and I've been lucky enough to be on the receiving end of it too, more times than I can count.

So today, on this small milestone that somehow always feels bigger than its name suggests, I want to say something simple: thank you. Thank you for staying, even when staying wasn't the easiest choice. Thank you for choosing me, again and again, in a world that gives people plenty of easy reasons to give up on each other instead.

And thank you, simply, for existing. I don't say that lightly. Your existence alone — the fact that you were born into this world, grew into who you are, and somehow ended up here, with me — has made my life fuller than I ever expected it to be. I don't take that for granted, not for a single day.

Here's to this month, and to every quiet, ordinary, beautiful one that comes after it. Here's to more mornings, more laughter, more of your patience and your presence. I love you, more than these words could ever fully hold.

Happy monthsary. 🤍.
